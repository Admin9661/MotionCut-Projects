Spaghetti Carbonara

A classic Italian pasta with pancetta, eggs, and cheese.

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 eggs
  • 50g parmesan
  • Salt & pepper
  1. Cook pasta in salted boiling water until al dente.
  2. Fry pancetta until crispy.
  3. Whisk eggs and cheese together in a bowl.
  4. Drain pasta and combine with pancetta.
  5. Remove from heat, add egg-cheese mixture, and toss quickly.
  6. Season with salt and pepper. Serve hot.

Margherita Pizza

Classic Italian pizza topped with fresh tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil.

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh mozzarella
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Add slices of fresh mozzarella cheese.
  5. Top with fresh basil leaves.
  6. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with salt.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.

Lasagna

Layered pasta with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese.

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Béchamel sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Spices
  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Prepare meat sauce by browning beef and adding tomato sauce and spices.
  4. Layer noodles, meat sauce, béchamel, and cheeses in a baking dish.
  5. Repeat layers and finish with cheese on top.
  6. Bake for 35-40 minutes until golden and bubbly.
  7. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving.

Risotto alla Milanese

Creamy saffron-infused risotto, a classic from Milan.

  • Arborio rice
  • Saffron threads
  • Chicken broth
  • Onion
  • Butter
  • Parmesan cheese
  • White wine
  1. Heat broth and keep warm; steep saffron in a little hot broth.
  2. Sauté chopped onion in butter until soft.
  3. Add Arborio rice and cook, stirring, until edges are translucent.
  4. Pour in white wine and stir until absorbed.
  5. Add saffron-infused broth and a ladle of hot broth; stir until absorbed.
  6. Continue adding broth, stirring, until rice is creamy and al dente.
  7. Stir in butter and Parmesan cheese before serving.
⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️☆ (4.4)

Enchiladas

Rolled tortillas filled with chicken and topped with spicy enchilada sauce.

  • Corn tortillas
  • Shredded chicken
  • Enchilada sauce
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Onions
  • Cilantro
  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Fill corn tortillas with shredded chicken.
  3. Roll up the filled tortillas and place them seam-side down in a baking dish.
  4. Pour enchilada sauce over the rolled tortillas.
  5. Sprinkle cheddar cheese and onions on top.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Garnish with cilantro and serve hot.

Chiles Rellenos

Poblano peppers stuffed with cheese, battered, and fried to perfection.

  • Poblano peppers
  • Monterey Jack cheese
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Tomato sauce
  • Spices
  1. Roast poblano peppers until skins blister, then peel and remove seeds.
  2. Stuff each pepper with Monterey Jack cheese.
  3. Separate eggs and beat whites until stiff, then fold in yolks to make batter.
  4. Dust stuffed peppers with flour, dip in egg batter.
  5. Fry peppers in hot oil until golden and crispy.
  6. Serve hot with tomato sauce and garnish as desired.
⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️☆ (4.1)

Paneer Butter Masala

Creamy curry made with paneer and spiced tomato gravy.

  • Paneer cubes
  • Tomato puree
  • Butter & cream
  • Spices
  • Onions
  1. Heat butter in a pan and sauté onions until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  3. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
  4. Mix in spices and cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Add paneer cubes and simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Stir in cream and cook until the gravy is creamy and thick.
  7. Garnish with coriander and serve hot.
⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️☆ (4.5)

Chicken Tikka Masala

Tender chicken pieces cooked in a creamy spiced tomato sauce.

  • Chicken breast
  • Yogurt
  • Tomato puree
  • Cream
  • Spices
  • Onions
  1. Marinate chicken pieces in yogurt and spices for at least 1 hour.
  2. Grill or pan-cook the marinated chicken until cooked through.
  3. Sauté onions, ginger, and garlic in butter until golden.
  4. Add tomato puree and cook with spices until oil separates.
  5. Add grilled chicken pieces to the sauce and simmer.
  6. Stir in cream and cook for a few more minutes.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with naan or rice.
⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️ (4.8)

Chole Bhature

Spicy chickpea curry served with fluffy fried bread.

  • Chickpeas
  • Onions & tomatoes
  • Spices
  • Flour
  • Yogurt
  • Oil
  1. Soak chickpeas overnight and pressure cook until soft.
  2. Sauté onions, tomatoes, and spices to make the gravy.
  3. Add cooked chickpeas to the gravy and simmer until thick.
  4. Prepare dough for bhature using flour, yogurt, and a pinch of salt.
  5. Roll out dough balls and deep-fry until golden and puffed.
  6. Serve hot chole with freshly fried bhature.

Masala Dosa

Crispy rice crepe filled with spiced potato mixture.

  • Rice & urad dal
  • Potatoes
  • Onions
  • Spices
  • Oil
  1. Soak rice and urad dal, then grind to a smooth batter and ferment overnight.
  2. Boil potatoes, mash, and sauté with onions, spices, and herbs to make the masala filling.
  3. Heat a non-stick pan, pour a ladle of batter, and spread into a thin circle.
  4. Drizzle oil around the edges and cook until golden and crisp.
  5. Place potato masala in the center, fold the dosa, and serve hot.

Dal Makhani

Rich and creamy black lentil curry slow-cooked with butter and cream.

  • Black lentils
  • Kidney beans
  • Tomatoes
  • Cream & butter
  • Spices
  • Onions
  1. Soak black lentils and kidney beans overnight.
  2. Pressure cook lentils and beans until soft.
  3. Sauté onions, tomatoes, and spices in butter.
  4. Add cooked lentils and beans to the masala.
  5. Simmer on low heat for 30-40 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Stir in cream and more butter before serving.
⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️☆ (4.7)
