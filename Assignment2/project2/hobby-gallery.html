About Me

My name is Shivanshu Kumar, and I am a highly motivated and dedicated Computer Science Engineering student.

I'm passionate about exploring new interests and dedicating time to my favorite hobbies. I enjoy painting, photography, and hiking in nature. In my free time, I also love reading novels, playing the guitar, and experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen. This gallery showcases some of the activities that inspire and excite me.

Painting

Painting allows me to express creativity and emotions through colors and brushstrokes. It helps me relax, focus, and see the world from new perspectives while creating something unique and personal.

Photography

Capturing and preserving special moments, scenes, and emotions through photography lets me explore creativity, notice details in everyday life, and share a unique perspective with others.

Hiking

Hiking connects me with nature, clears my mind, and keeps me active. Exploring new trails and landscapes brings a sense of adventure and appreciation for the beauty of the outdoors.

Reading Novels

Novels transport me to different worlds, spark imagination, and offer diverse perspectives. Reading is a way to relax, expand knowledge, and develop empathy by connecting with characters and their stories.

Playing The Guitar

Playing the guitar is a creative outlet that helps me relax and improve focus. Learning new songs and techniques brings a sense of accomplishment and provides opportunities to connect with others through music.

Try New Recipes

Experimenting with new recipes encourages creativity in the kitchen and the discovery of new flavors. Cooking is a relaxing activity that allows me to share delicious meals with family and friends, making each new recipe an opportunity to learn and enjoy the process.
